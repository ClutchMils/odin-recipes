Caramelized Banana

Home

Description

Caramelized bananas are a simple yet delicious dessert that brings out the natural sweetness of ripe bananas. Perfect for topping pancakes, waffles, or enjoying on their own!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Â¼ cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: cinnamon for sprinkling

Steps

  1. In a skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced bananas to the skillet and cook for about 2 minutes, until they start to soften.
  3. Sprinkle the brown sugar over the bananas and stir gently to coat them evenly.
  4. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the sugar to melt and caramelize, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt, stirring to combine.
  6. If desired, sprinkle a little cinnamon over the top for extra flavor.
  7. Remove from heat and serve warm. Enjoy your caramelized bananas on pancakes, waffles, or on their own!
